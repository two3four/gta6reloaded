The State of Leonida

Explore the diverse landscapes of the biggest GTA map ever created.

Vice City

The neon-soaked heart of Leonida. From the sun-drenched beaches to the high-rise financial district, Vice City is where the money flows and the crime never sleeps.

Leonida Keys

A string of tropical islands connected by massive bridges. Perfect for smuggling and high-speed boat chases through crystal clear waters.

The Everglades

Vast wetlands teeming with dangerous wildlife and hidden meth labs. The perfect place to disappear if you've got a 5-star wanted level.

Leonida Penitentiary

A high-security facility housing the state's most dangerous criminals. Rumored to be the starting point for Lucia's story.

Port Leonida

The industrial backbone of the state. Huge container ships, cranes, and narrow warehouse alleys provide the ultimate playground for urban warfare.

State of Gloriana

The neighboring state mentioned in the leaks, potentially offering a completely different atmosphere from the sun-soaked Leonida.
