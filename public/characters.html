Characters

LUCIA CAMINOS
Protagonist

One of the two main protagonists. Lucia is fierce, street-smart, and deeply protective of her loved ones. After serving a prison sentence, she returns to the neon-drenched streets of Leonida determined to secure a better life by any means necessary.

JASON DUVAL
Protagonist

The male co-protagonist of GTA 6. Partnered with Lucia, Jason brings heavy muscle, tactical gunplay, and precision driving to their crew. Though he seeks a quick road to wealth, he constantly finds himself pulled deeper into the dangerous cartel networks of Leonida.

DRE'QUAN PRIEST
Street Connection

An aspiring music producer and partner at Only Raw Records. Dre'Quan represents the new wave of the Vice City scene, acting as a critical gateway to complex smuggling routes and street hierarchies.

RAUL BAUTISTA
Enforcer

A seasoned and veteran bank robber known for taking massive risks for legendary scores. Raul represents the old-school muscle of Vice City's cartel scene, commanding absolute respect through ruthless efficiency.

CAL HAMPTON
Conspiracy Theorist

A rampant conspiracy theorist and longtime friend of Jason. Operating out of the shadows of the city's elite, Cal provides critical intelligence and shakedown opportunities to the team.

REAL DIMEZ
Viral Influencers

The viral Florida rap duo taking Leonida by storm. Famous for their spicy tracks and social media antics, Bae-Luxe and Roxy leverage their massive digital following to aid the crew's operations.

BOOBIE IKE
Racer & Mogul

A legendary racer on the Vice City street circuit and a prominent nightclub mogul. Boobie Ike knows every secret shortcut and alleyway in the city, controlling Leonida's high-speed night life.

BRIAN HEDER
Keys Smuggler

A veteran smuggler operating from the Florida Keys boatyards. Brian manages waterfront logistics and shakedowns, offering Jason a place to hide in exchange for muscle.

STEFANIE
Prison Counselor

A dedicated prison counselor and correctional social worker. Stefanie oversees Lucia's parole transition, representing the fragile barrier between freedom and returning to the criminal underworld.
