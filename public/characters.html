Characters

LUCIA CAMINOS
Protagonists

The core duo of GTA 6. Lucia and Jason bring a modern "Bonnie & Clyde" dynamic to the state of Leonida. Their story is one of loyalty, high-stakes crime, and survival in a world that's always watching.

JASON DUVAL
Key Contact

A high-stakes fixer in Vice City who provides the duo with their first major scores. Cal operates in the shadows of the city's elite, bridging the gap between legitimate business and the criminal underworld.

DRE'QUAN PRIEST
Street Connection

A rising star in the Leonida music scene with deep ties to the streets. Dre'Quan is more than just a musician; he's a gateway to the city's complex gang hierarchies and smuggling routes.

RAUL BAUTISTA
Enforcer

Raul is a formidable presence in the Vice City cartel scene. Known for his ruthless efficiency, he represents the old-school muscle that still commands respect in the modern era.

BOOBIE IKE
Legendary Racer

A legend in the Vice City street racing circuit and a nightclub owner. Boobie Ike knows every alleyway and every secret of the city's night life.

BRIAN HEDER
Tech Expert

The digital architect of the team. Brian handles the high-tech side of their heists, from hacking security systems to managing encrypted communications.
